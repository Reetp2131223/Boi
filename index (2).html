HOLD THE LINE

A tug-of-war frontline command game. Your treasury fills automatically — spend it deploying units that march toward the enemy base and fight whatever stands in their way. Capture the flags in the middle of the field to boost your income, and upgrade your Command HQ to unlock stronger units.

  • Units move and fight automatically — there is no direct unit control.
  • Click a unit button to deploy it. It costs money and marches out immediately.
  • Hold a capture flag by keeping more units near it than the enemy.
  • Destroy the enemy Command HQ before they destroy yours.

VICTORY

The enemy Command HQ has fallen.
